The Scarlet Devil: Remilia Scarlet

Remilia Scarlet is the owner of Scarlet Devil Mansion. She is a noble vampire presumebly older than 500 years.

She is almost always accompanied by her human maid named Izayoi Sakuya, who has misterious ability to manipulate time.

She is not native to Gensokyo but settled down after losing the great yokai war.

Powers

Here are some of Remilia Scarlet's powers mentioned in no particular order.

  • Ability to Control Fate
  • Vampiric Strength
  • Vampiric Speed
  • Vampiric Endurance
